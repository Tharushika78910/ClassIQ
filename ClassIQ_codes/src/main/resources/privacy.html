Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ClassIQ – Teacher’s Gradebook and Report Card System collects, uses, and protects user information. ClassIQ is developed as an academic project for educational purposes.

1. Information We Collect

ClassIQ may collect and store the following information:

  • Teacher account information (username and password)
  • Student details (student name, student ID, grades, assignments, exam marks)
  • Academic performance data used to calculate averages and final grades

2. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used solely for managing student grades, calculating weighted results, generating report cards, and providing secure login access for authorized users.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely within the system. Access is restricted through role-based authentication. Basic security measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access to academic records.

4. Data Sharing

ClassIQ does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. The system is designed for educational use within a controlled academic environment.

5. User Rights

Teachers can manage and update student records within the system. Students can view their academic performance securely through their accounts.

6. Cookies and Tracking

ClassIQ is a desktop-based application and does not use cookies or online tracking technologies.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project evolves. Updates will be reflected within the system documentation.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the project development team.
